Laptop

Laptops contain all of the computer's parts in a small package that is designed for optimal performance without requiring too much storage space. The only two components of a standard foldable laptop are the laptop itself and the detachable laptop charger.

Computer

Computer information systems are the technology-based application or solution to accomplish the business needs of an organization. Basically, the main components of computer information systems are software, hardware, databases, networks, and people.
